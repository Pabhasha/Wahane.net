BMW 740 LE

This is the BMW 740Le, which roughly translates as ‘BMW 7-Series hybrid’. The ‘e’ drivetrain twins a 2-litre four-cylinder petrol engine with an electric motor, for a total output of 326bhp and 369lb ft. You can spec it with the standard length 7-Series, as a rear-wheel-drive 740e, or as a long-wheelbase 7-Series, this all-wheel-drive 740Le.
