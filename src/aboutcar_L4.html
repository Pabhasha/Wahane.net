Mercedes Benz S400

The Mercedes Benz S Class S400 is a premium level sedan as the flagship luxury car line up. Mercedes easily sets up styling benchmarks for competitors with this model. The Mercedes Benz S-Class S 400 is powered by a 2996 cc engine which generates a maximum power of 329 bhp @ 5250 rpm and generates a maximum torque of 480 Nm @ 1600 rpm. The luxury sedan runs on a 7G-TRONIC, 7 speed automatic gearbox with a top speed of 250 kmph and a boot space of 530 litres. It is a segment rival to Audi A8 and BMW 7 series in the segment.
