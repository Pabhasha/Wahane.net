Toyota KP 30

Toyota Corolla KP30 also known as Toyota Publica is a classic car with a reliable engine. This is the 1st generation of Toyota Starlet, and this car is manufactured in 1972. This car has a 1000cc inline 4-cylinder engine which is good for day-to-day driving. Toyota Corolla KP30 is a 2-door fastback and the specialty of this model is that even though it is manufactured in Japan, it is made for Canada and US markets.
