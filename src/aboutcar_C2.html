Toyota KP 30

This is probably the most original, untouchedToyota Publica 1000 (KP30) 1974' in the world. This is the second generation Publica with a 1000 cc (2K) motor and a 4-speed gearbox. Derived from "public" and "car," the name signifies "a car cherished by the public." The name was chosen in a public naming competition that attracted nearly 1.1 million entries. The car is in excellent-original condition inside and out.
